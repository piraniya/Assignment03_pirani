  • + Why flowers are falling off or dying?

    • Flowers have a natural life cycle, and it is normal for them to wilt and fall off as they age.
    This is a natural part of the plant's reproductive process.

  • + What causes leaves to become pale?

    • Inadequate soil pH or poorly draining soil can affect nutrient availability to the plant,
    leading to yellowing leaves.

  • + What causes brown crispy leaves?

    • Exposure to chemicals, such as herbicides or pesticides, can lead to leaf damage and browning.

  • + How do i choose a plant?

    • Consider the available space in your home or garden.
    Larger plants or those with spreading habits may not be suitable for small spaces.

  • + How do i change the pots?

    • Water the plant thoroughly after transplanting.
    This helps settle the soil and ensures good contact between the roots and the new potting mix.

  • + Why are gnats flying around my plant?

    • Ensure that your plant's pot has proper drainage holes and that the soil is well-draining.
